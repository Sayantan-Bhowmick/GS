About Us

GirlScript foundation is a non profitable organisation which was initiated by Anubha Maheshwar in 2017. Originated in Nagpur, Maharashtra it has come a long way spreading itself throughout the country with the motive of empowering girls in the field of computer science and IT by providing 50% reservations to girls and LGBQT people in all its events held till date. Girlscript Siliguri chapter along with its members was started in 2019 and has worked to establish it's feet in the city as well as regions around it creating a wide network of students in and around and wishes to welcome more of them. Spreading its sheet with the official social media sites and the regular contests being held in it, we have received enormous participation till date from people of different region.
